Web Design Specialist

Job description:

•	Collaborate with clients and stakeholders to understand project requirements and objectives.
•	Conceptualize and create visually appealing, intuitive, and responsive website designs.
•	Develop and maintain a consistent brand identity and design language across web platforms.
•	Work closely with UX/UI designers to ensure a seamless and user-friendly design experience.
•	Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes to effectively communicate design concepts.
•	Use design tools such as Adobe Creative Suite, Sketch, or Figma to bring design ideas to life.
•	Implement front-end design elements using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
•	Ensure cross-browser compatibility and responsiveness of web designs.
•	Collaborate with developers to implement design specifications and provide support during the development process.
•	Conduct usability testing and gather feedback to iterate on design concepts.
•	Stay updated on industry trends and emerging design technologies.
•	Maintain and update existing websites, ensuring design consistency and relevance.
•	Collaborate with marketing teams to create visually compelling assets for online campaigns.
•	Provide design support for various digital platforms, including social media and email campaigns.
