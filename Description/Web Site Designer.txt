Web Site Designer

Job description:

•	Execute all visual design stages from concept to final hand-off to engineer.
•	Conceptualize original website design ideas that bring simplicity and user friendliness to complex roadblocks.
•	Create wireframes, user flows and site maps to communicate interaction and design ideas.
•	Present and defend designs and key deliverables to peers and executive level stakeholders.
•	Establish and promote design guidelines, best practices and standards.
•	Work closely with web developers to implement the design into a functional website, ensuring that the design is translated accurately and efficiently.
•	Stay informed about the latest trends and technologies in web design and apply them to enhance the quality and features of websites.
•	The candidate should have atleast 1 year of work experience as a Web Designer.
•	Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Responsive for rapid prototyping.
•	Demonstrable graphic design or Visual Design skills with a strong portfolio.
•	Excellent understanding of digital design principles, color theory, and user experience (UX).
•	Solid experience in creating wireframes, user flows, process flows and site maps.
•	Proficiency in Photoshop, Illustrator or other visual design and wire-framing tools like Figma, Adobe XD.
•	Excellent visual design skills with sensitivity to user-system interaction.
•	Ability to solve problems creatively and effectively.
•	Well Known knowledge For Design Systems.
•	Strong knowledge of HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap 4 and 5, Basic VueJS, jQuery.
