Full Stack Developer

Job description:

•	Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand project requirements and contribute to the design and architecture of full-stack solutions.
•	Develop and maintain server-side logic, databases, and APIs to ensure seamless integration with front-end components.
•	Design and implement scalable, responsive, and user-friendly web applications using front-end technologies such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
•	Implement and maintain security and data protection measures across the full stack.
•	Collaborate with UX/UI designers to implement design concepts and ensure a cohesive and visually appealing user experience.
•	Write well-documented, reusable, and efficient code for both front-end and back-end components.
•	Conduct thorough testing and debugging to identify and resolve issues, ensuring high-quality code.
•	Stay abreast of emerging technologies and industry trends, incorporating them into development processes.
•	Participate in code reviews to maintain code quality, share knowledge, and provide constructive feedback.
•	Work closely with other team members, including other developers, designers, and product managers, to deliver high-quality software solutions.
