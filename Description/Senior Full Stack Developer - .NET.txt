**Senior Full Stack Developer - .NET**

**Job Description**

**About the position:**

The Sr. Developer has primary responsibility for the following:

- Working with clients to understand their business problems and proposing solutions.
- Managing multiple in-flight projects with different timelines and utilizing various technologies.
- Working within and managing a source control environment and mentoring new developers in this environment.

**Required Skills**

The successful candidate will possess the following skills and experience:

- Strong communication skills in dealing with IT business units and C-Level leadership.
- Ability to mentor and take responsibility for onshore and offshore team members’ deliverables towards project goals.
- Hands-on experience with development tools and frameworks.
- 5+ years’ experience with the .NET Framework (version 3.5 or higher).
- Solid understanding of ASP.NET MVC, Web API, ADO.NET (Entity Framework).
- 5+ years’ experience developing web-based applications in .NET Framework.
- 5+ years’ experience developing and implementing SQL scripts and stored procedures in SQL Server.
- 3+ years’ experience working in a source-controlled environment, such as Git or TFS.
- Ability to implement coding best practices and standards.
- Solid understanding of best practices with web application design.
- Solid ability to understand business value of requirements and prioritize requirements relative to development effort; ability to communicate this prioritization to business units and IT leadership.

**Desirable Skills**

- 5+ years’ experience with C#.
- 3-5 years’ experience with JavaScript.
- 2-3 years’ experience with client-side frameworks like Bootstrap or Angular (5 and above).
- Technology modernization through cloud architecture migration experience.
- Familiarity with NoSQL systems like MongoDB or Redis.
- Deep understanding of Microservices.
- Knowledge in 12-factor application architecture.
- Solid understanding of various Design Patterns.
- Work effort estimation skills.
- Understanding of Agile software development principles along with experience participating in agile projects.

**Education Qualification:** Master of Computer Applications from Sri Venkateswara University, 2008.

**Project Types:** Rights Line, COMPASS Express, Contract Management & Program Accounting Scheduling System (COMPASS), Participations and Residual Information System (PARIS), Time Keeper.

**Certification:** MCSD (Microsoft Certified Solutions Developer)
