Front End Engineer

Job description:

•	Develop and implement responsive and intuitive user interfaces.
•	Collaborate with design teams to bring visual concepts to life.
•	Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
•	Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
•	Stay informed about emerging front-end technologies and best practices.
•	Collaborate with back-end developers to integrate user-facing elements.
•	Troubleshoot and resolve front-end issues and bugs.
•	Proven experience as a Front-End Developer or similar role.
•	Strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
•	Experience with front-end frameworks such as React, Angular, or Vue.js.
•	Knowledge of web performance optimization and responsive design.
